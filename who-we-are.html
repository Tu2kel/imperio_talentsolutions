Anthony K. Kelley Sr.

Founder & CEO | 21-Year Army/Navy Veteran (E-7 Retired)

Imperio Talent Solutions was founded on a simple premise: the same discipline, accountability, and mission-first execution that drives military operations should drive how businesses source talent and fulfill supply requirements.

With 21 years of combined Army and Navy service as an S4 Supply NCOIC, Anthony managed over $1.5 billion in equipment and supply accountability, led 70+ personnel, and coordinated procurement operations across multiple theaters.

Imperio operates as a division of The House of Kel LLC, headquartered in Killeen, Texas, near Fort Cavazos.
