What We Do

Imperio Talent Solutions is a dual-capability SDVOSB providing recruiter-only talent sourcing and wholesale product fulfillment for government agencies, prime contractors, and defense organizations. We place cleared professionals and deliver TAA-compliant supplies under one contract vehicle.

IT & Cybersecurity

Cleared IT professionals and TAA-compliant hardware. From TS/SCI talent to laptops and networking equipment.

Medical & Healthcare

Healthcare staffing and medical supply fulfillment. PPE, surgical supplies, and clinical professionals.

HVAC & Trades

HVAC technicians, maintenance professionals, and parts/equipment procurement for government facilities.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Workforce solutions and office/operational supplies. The backbone that keeps missions running.

Our Story

Who We Are

Anthony K. Kelley Sr.

Founder & CEO · Retired Army / Navy Veteran E-7 · S4 NCOIC

Anthony Kelley is a 21-year military veteran with deep experience in large-scale logistics, supply chain management, and workforce leadership. As a former S4 NCOIC he managed over $1.5 billion in equipment and supplies, led 70+ personnel, and executed procurement cycles under high-stakes operational conditions.

He founded Imperio Talent Solutions as a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business to bring that same discipline, accountability, and precision to government talent acquisition and product fulfillment. Based in Killeen, Texas minutes from Fort Cavazos Imperio is built to serve the military and federal community from the inside out.

Direct Hire

Full-time permanent placements. One-time placement fee. Candidate is your employee from day one.

Contract-to-Hire

Trial period on contract before converting to full-time. Reduces hiring risk while maintaining flexibility.

Contract Staffing

Project-based or term-limited assignments. Flexible workforce scaling for surge requirements.

Payroll & Factoring Contract Staffing Enabled

Imperio supports contract staffing engagements through PO-backed fulfillment and payroll factoring. We can carry payroll on active contract placements without requiring upfront capital from the client — enabling faster starts, cleaner invoicing, and scalable staffing under government purchase orders. Net-30 terms available for qualifying agencies and prime contractors.

CONTRACT VEHICLES

Multiple pathways for government agencies, primes, and education buyers to procure staffing and supply services from Imperio.

Federal
Available to all federal agencies and approved contractors
Active

SDVOSB Sole Source

Direct award to Imperio without competition. Available to any federal contracting officer for contracts up to $5M.

Authority: FAR 19.1405  |  Ceiling: $5M
Active

SDVOSB Set-Aside

Competitive procurement restricted to SDVOSB vendors. Imperio competes against a smaller, qualified pool.

Authority: FAR 19.1405  |  Type: Competitive
Active

Micro-Purchase (Open Market)

Direct purchase with a Government Purchase Card (GPC). No competition required. Fast, simple transactions.

Threshold: Up to $10,000  |  Payment: GPC Card
Active

SAM.gov Open Market

Registered and searchable on SAM.gov for direct award and competitive opportunities across all federal agencies.
