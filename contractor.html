What We Do

Imperio Talent Solutions is a dual-capability SDVOSB providing recruiter-only talent sourcing and wholesale product fulfillment for government agencies, prime contractors, and defense organizations. We place cleared professionals and deliver TAA-compliant supplies — under one contract vehicle.

IT & Cybersecurity

Cleared IT professionals and TAA-compliant hardware. From TS/SCI talent to laptops and networking equipment.

Medical & Healthcare

Healthcare staffing and medical supply fulfillment. PPE, surgical supplies, and clinical professionals.

HVAC & Trades

HVAC technicians, maintenance professionals, and parts/equipment procurement for government facilities.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Workforce solutions and office/operational supplies. The backbone that keeps missions running.

Anthony K. Kelley Sr.

Founder & CEO  |  21-Year Army/Navy Veteran (E-7 Retired)

Imperio Talent Solutions was founded on a simple premise: the same discipline, accountability, and mission-first execution that drives military operations should drive how businesses source talent and fulfill supply requirements.

With 21 years of combined Army and Navy service as an S4 Supply NCOIC, Anthony managed over $1.5 billion in equipment and supply accountability, led 70+ personnel, and coordinated procurement operations across multiple theaters. From ammunition handling to large-scale inventory management, his career was built on making sure the right resources reached the right people — on time, every time.

That operational DNA is now the foundation of Imperio. Whether placing a cleared cybersecurity analyst at a DoD installation or fulfilling a $10K office supply order for a VA medical center, the approach is the same: clean communication, predictable follow-through, and zero overpromising.

Imperio operates as a division of The House of Kel LLC, headquartered in Killeen, Texas, near Fort Cavazos — one of the largest military installations in the world.

Staffing Solutions

Recruiter-only talent sourcing across four critical verticals

Imperio delivers pre-vetted, qualified candidates through a recruiter-only model — no temp agencies, no body shops. We source, screen, and present professionals who are ready to contribute from day one. Our specialization in cleared and credentialed talent means faster placements with less risk.

IT & Cybersecurity

Network engineers, cybersecurity analysts, systems administrators, help desk professionals, cloud architects. Secret, TS, and TS/SCI cleared candidates available.

HVAC & Trades

HVAC technicians, maintenance specialists, facilities engineers, and skilled trades professionals for government installations and commercial facilities.

Medical & Healthcare

Logistics & Supply Chain

Warehouse managers, logistics coordinators, procurement specialists, inventory control analysts, and transportation professionals.

Procurement & Supply

TAA-compliant product fulfillment for government and prime contractor buyers

Imperio sources and delivers products through authorized wholesale distributors with established TAA-compliant product lines. All products meet federal Trade Agreements Act requirements. We offer drop-ship direct to your facility, quote-to-delivery in 1–5 business days, and accept GPC cards for micro-purchases under $10K.

IT Equipment

Laptops, desktops, monitors, peripherals, networking equipment, cables, and accessories. All TAA-compliant through authorized distributors.

Office Supplies

Administrative materials, stationery, toner, paper, small equipment, breakroom supplies, and operational consumables.

Medical & PPE

Gloves, gowns, masks, surgical supplies, safety equipment, first aid, and infection control products.

HVAC & Facilities

HVAC parts, filters, maintenance supplies, janitorial products, safety gear, and MRO (Maintenance, Repair & Operations) materials.

SDVOSB Sole Source

Direct award to Imperio without competition. Available to any federal contracting officer for contracts up to $5M.

SDVOSB Set-Aside

Competitive procurement restricted to SDVOSB vendors. Imperio competes against a smaller, qualified pool.
